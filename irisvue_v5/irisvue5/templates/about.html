About Irisvue

Your cinematic
view of the world.

Irisvue is a film discovery and logging platform built for people who truly love cinema. It goes beyond simple ratings — giving you the full picture of every film, from the camera and lens used to shoot it, to the locations it was filmed in, the full cast and technical crew, and the most iconic cinematographic shots.

The name says it all. Iris — the part of the camera and the human eye that controls how much light enters. Vue — the French word for view. Together: your cinematic view of the world.

Built with the belief that every film deserves to be understood as deeply as it is felt.
